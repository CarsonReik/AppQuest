Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2, 2026

Welcome to AppQuest. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Display name (username)
  • Profile picture (if provided)
  • Authentication credentials
  • Account creation date

If you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account email, display name, and profile photo.

1.2 Profile and Preferences

We store your in-app preferences and settings, including:

  • User mode (user, developer, or both)
  • Favorite app categories
  • Notification preferences (streak reminders, quest updates, social activity, new content)
  • Onboarding completion status

1.3 User-Generated Content

We collect content you create and share within the app:

  • Quest Submissions: Screenshots you upload as proof of quest completion. These screenshots may contain personal information visible on your device.
  • Forum Posts and Replies: Discussion content you post in community forums.
  • Comments: Reviews and comments you leave on app listings.
  • Reports: Details you provide when reporting content or users.
  • Disputes: Information you submit when disputing a quest denial, including explanations and supporting screenshots.
  • Refund Requests: Reasons and screenshots provided when requesting code refunds.

1.4 Social Data

When you use social features, we collect:

  • Follow relationships (who you follow and who follows you)
  • Users you have blocked
  • Likes on posts, comments, and apps
  • Your watchlist and favorited apps

1.5 Activity and Engagement Data

We automatically collect information about your activity:

  • Apps viewed, liked, and installed
  • Quests completed and points earned
  • Codes unlocked and points spent
  • Daily and weekly streak progress
  • Achievements unlocked and level progression
  • Leaderboard rankings
  • Last active timestamp

1.6 Developer Data

If you use developer features, we additionally collect:

  • App submissions (name, description, screenshots, icons, store links)
  • Quest configurations you create
  • Promotional codes you generate
  • Analytics on your apps (views, completions, code claims)
  • Promotion purchase history

1.7 Device and Technical Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token for push notifications
  • App version

1.8 Purchase Information

When you make in-app purchases (subscriptions, quest slots, point packs, promotions), payment processing is handled by Google Play and RevenueCat. We receive:

  • Confirmation of purchases
  • Subscription status and expiration dates
  • Product identifiers purchased

We do not store your payment card details.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Public Information

The following information is visible to other users:

  • Display name and profile picture
  • User level and achievements
  • Forum posts and replies
  • Comments on app listings
  • Leaderboard rankings
  • Follower/following counts

3.2 Shared with Developers

When you complete quests, app developers can see:

  • Your display name and profile picture
  • Your submitted screenshots
  • Submission timestamps

3.3 Shared with Moderators/Admins

Our moderation team may access your data to:

  • Review reported content
  • Resolve disputes
  • Investigate policy violations
  • Process refund requests

3.4 Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf (see Section 4).

3.5 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or valid government request.

3.6 Business Transfers

In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

4.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages user sign-in and account security

  • Firebase Cloud Functions: Processes background tasks

Privacy Policy: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

4.2 RevenueCat

Manages subscriptions and in-app purchases.

Privacy Policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

4.3 Google Play

Processes payments for in-app purchases.

Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

4.4 Google Sign-In

Provides OAuth authentication.

Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

4.5 Google AdMob

May display advertisements within the app.

Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active
  • User-generated content: Retained until deleted by you or removed for policy violations
  • Quest submissions: Retained for dispute resolution purposes
  • Activity logs: Retained for analytics and fraud prevention
  • Deleted accounts: Data is deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law

6. Data Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Firebase security rules restricting data access
  • Authentication requirements for sensitive operations
  • Regular security reviews

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Portability

You can request a copy of your personal data by contacting us.

7.2 Correction

You can update your profile information directly in the app settings.

7.3 Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. Some data may be retained as required by law.

7.4 Notification Preferences

You can control push notification settings within the app.

7.5 Content Removal

You can delete your own forum posts, comments, and other content through the app.

7.6 Blocking

You can block other users to prevent them from interacting with you.

8. Children's Privacy

AppQuest is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence, including the United States where our service providers operate. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using AppQuest, you consent to such transfers.

10. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
