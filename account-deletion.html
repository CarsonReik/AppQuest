AppQuest Account & Data Deletion

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how to delete your AppQuest account and what happens to your data when you do.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Delete from the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the AppQuest app
  2. Go to Profile (bottom navigation)
  3. Tap the Settings icon (gear)
  4. Scroll down to Danger Zone
  5. Tap Delete Account
  6. Confirm by following the prompts

Option 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by email:

  1. Send an email to support@compr.co
  2. Use the subject line: Delete My AppQuest Account
  3. Include the email address associated with your account
  4. We will process your request within 30 days
Request Account Deletion

What Data is Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

Data Type Action
Account information (email, display name) Deleted
Profile picture Deleted
Points and achievements Deleted
Streak history Deleted
Quest submissions and screenshots Deleted
Forum posts and replies Deleted
Comments on apps Deleted
Likes and favorites Deleted
Followers and following Deleted
Notification preferences Deleted
Code unlock history Deleted

What Data May Be Retained

Some data may be retained for legal, security, or business purposes:

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Purchase/transaction records Up to 7 years Tax and legal compliance
Reports you filed against others Up to 1 year Safety and abuse prevention
Reports filed against you Up to 1 year Safety and abuse prevention

Important: This Action Cannot Be Undone

Once your account is deleted, all your data is permanently removed and cannot be recovered. This includes:

  • All earned points and achievements
  • Your streak progress
  • Any unlocked codes (codes cannot be refunded after deletion)
  • Developer apps and analytics (if applicable)

Active subscriptions will not be automatically cancelled. Please cancel your subscription through Google Play before deleting your account.

Delete Specific Data (Without Deleting Account)

If you want to remove specific data without deleting your entire account, you can:

  • Delete forum posts: Tap the menu on your post and select "Delete"
  • Delete comments: Tap the menu on your comment and select "Delete"
  • Remove profile picture: Go to Settings > Change Avatar > Remove Avatar
  • Clear favorites/watchlist: Remove items individually from your lists

Questions?
